Cakes

Butterscotch

This butterscotch cake is made with moist and tender brown sugar cake layers, homemade butterscotch sauce, and topped and filled with butterscotch buttercream. This cake is topped with more butterscotch sauce and is smoothed out with a spatula creating a marble design.

Hazelnut-Butter Cake

Supersoft and moist hazelnut butter cake made with freshly toasted and ground hazelnuts. This hazelnut cake is frosted with a hazelnut penuche frosting, a brown sugar frosting mixed with freshly ground hazelnut paste giving it absolutely excellent flavor.
